# Health Story Collaborative Submission Package

Status: prepared, not sent.

Use only after a real story owner and contributor consent are ready.

## Source-Checked Route

- Share Your Story page: `https://www.healthstorycollaborative.org/share-your-story`
- Email: `info@healthstorycollaborative.org`
- Best FinalNotes target: `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-stories/`

## Core Ask

Ask whether a consent-backed visual obituary story or short process reflection could fit Health Story Collaborative's story, blog, audio, visual art, or digital storytelling surfaces.

## Fit Notes

The strongest angle is not "obituary product." The strongest angle is the human story of navigating illness, caregiving, loss, or memory, and the moment when a family or caregiver turns health facts into a fuller life story.

## Materials To Prepare

- Completed narrative, story note, or interview proposal.
- Consent summary.
- Patient/privacy handling note if any clinical details appear.
- Visual-art or image-rights confirmation.
- Contributor bio.
- Context link: `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-stories/`

## Do Not Send Until

- The story is specific and human.
- The contributor has approved publication or inquiry language.
- Any patient, caregiver, family, or identifying details are permissioned or anonymized.
- The piece can stand on its own without needing a product pitch.
