Use only with a credible funeral-service author, subject-matter expert, or co-author.

## Core Ask

Query an original, unpublished article for The Director about how funeral professionals can help families turn obituary articles into source-backed, privacy-aware family memory.

## Best Angle

The article should treat obituary articles as a service-quality and family-care issue, not as SEO or software. FinalNotes can appear as one current example of source-backed visual obituary storytelling.

## Materials To Prepare

- Funeral-service author bio or co-author.
- 150-250 word query or short outline.
- 2-3 practical takeaways for funeral homes.
- Relevant clips or professional credentials.
- Context link: `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-articles/`

## Do Not Send Until

- The author can credibly address funeral-service professionals.
- The article is original and unpublished.
- The pitch is practical, tightly focused, and non-promotional.
- Any requested deadline/theme from the current editorial calendar is checked.
