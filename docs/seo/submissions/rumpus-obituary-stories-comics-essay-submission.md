# The Rumpus Submission Package

Status: prepared, not submitted.

## Source-Checked Route

- Guidelines/about page: `https://therumpus.net/about/`
- Submission manager: `https://therumpus.submittable.com/`
- Important rule: The Rumpus accepts submissions through Submittable and does not accept pitches.
- Important rule: Submittable displays only open categories; if a category is missing, it is paused.
- Best FinalNotes target: `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-stories/`

## Core Ask

Submit a full human essay or comics piece about the moment an obituary becomes a richer story, only when an appropriate Rumpus category is open.

## Best Angle

This should be a literary essay or comics submission about grief, memory, evidence, and one chosen image or scene. The piece has to stand alone as creative nonfiction or comics, not as brand coverage.

## Materials To Prepare

- Full unpublished essay or comics piece.
- Category-specific cover note.
- Author bio.
- Any required file format from the open Submittable category.
- Context link only if the contributor-note field permits it: `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-stories/`

## Do Not Submit Until

- A relevant category is open in Submittable.
- The piece is fully human and unpublished.
- The category allows the format.
- The FinalNotes link is allowed as context or bio only.
