# Public Books Submission Package

Status: prepared, not sent.

Use only with a concise cultural-criticism pitch and a real author bio.

## Source-Checked Route

- Submission page: `https://www.publicbooks.org/submissions/`
- Email: `editorial@publicbooks.org`
- Best FinalNotes target: `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-articles/`

## Core Ask

Pitch an essay about obituary articles as public interfaces for memory, evidence, privacy, authorship, local journalism, and digital memorial platforms.

## Best Angle

Obituary articles are small but consequential cultural forms. They decide which facts become public, which details stay private, and how a life is made legible to strangers. FinalNotes can appear as one contemporary example of a source-backed visual obituary system.

## Materials To Prepare

- Pitch under 500 words.
- Author bio and clips.
- Clear thesis.
- Why necessary / why now / why this author.
- Context link in parentheses only: `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-articles/`

## Do Not Send Until

- The pitch is under 500 words.
- It is not a project announcement.
- It can be read as cultural criticism.
- The author has a credible reason to make the argument.
