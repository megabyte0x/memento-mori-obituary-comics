## Core Ask

Pitch a JSTOR-grounded public-scholarship piece about obituary articles as public memory, family record, and archival evidence. Do not submit a promotional essay about FinalNotes.

## Best Angle

Obituary articles are small public memory interfaces. They preserve facts, omit private details, translate grief into public record, and later become sources for students, genealogists, journalists, and historians. FinalNotes can appear as one contemporary example of source-backed obituary storytelling.
