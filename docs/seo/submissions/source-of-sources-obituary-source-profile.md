# Source Of Sources Submission Package

Status: prepared, not subscribed.

## Source-Checked Route

- Home/sign-up page: `https://www.sourceofsources.com/`
- The page says sources receive journalist queries up to three times a day.
- It instructs sources to respond only when they have something productive, relevant, timely, and accurate to add.

## Signup Fields

- First name.
- Last name.
- Email.
- Email opt-in for daily media queries.

## Query Fit

Respond only to:

- obituary writing and family memorial stories
- funeral planning and digital memorialization
- AI obituary tools and sensitive content review
- family history, genealogy, archives, and death notices
- privacy, scams, copied obituaries, and memorial-link safety
- visual biography, memorial art, and source-backed storytelling

## Default Response Link Order

1. `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-articles/`
2. `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-stories/`
3. `https://www.finalnotes.page/what-not-to-include-in-an-obituary/`
4. `https://www.finalnotes.page/free-obituary-generator/`

## Do Not Subscribe Until

- The real sender approves receiving up to three media-query emails per day.
- The response owner can reply quickly and selectively.
- The sender accepts that most responses will not become links.
