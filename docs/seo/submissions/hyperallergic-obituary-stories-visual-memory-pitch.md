# Hyperallergic Submission Package

Status: prepared, not sent.

## Source-Checked Route

- Pitch guide: `https://hyperallergic.com/how-to-pitch/`
- Contact: `pitch@hyperallergic.com`
- Required subject pattern: `PITCH: [Category] - [Subject Matter]`
- Relevant categories: Opinion, Features, Remembrances & Obituaries.
- Best FinalNotes target: `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-stories/`

## Core Ask

Pitch an art/culture essay about obituary stories as visual memory work.

## Best Angle

Treat visual obituary stories as an emerging public-memory form. The essay should discuss how evidence, image choice, grief, authorship, and public display shape the way a life is represented after death.

## Materials To Prepare

- Working title.
- 2-3 sentence core argument.
- Why-now paragraph.
- Estimated word count.
- Image access and rights notes.
- Author bio and 2-3 clips.
- Conflict-of-interest disclosure if the author is connected to FinalNotes.
- Context link: `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-stories/`

## Do Not Send Until

- The pitch clearly fits Hyperallergic's art/culture audience.
- The author can disclose any relationship to FinalNotes.
- Image rights are known.
- The piece is not framed as product coverage.
