## Draft Pitch Angle

Working title: "What obituary articles reveal about everyday historical practice"

The post argues that obituary articles are everyday historical practice because they select sources, compress lives, negotiate public/private boundaries, and shape future discoverability. It should connect family memory, archives, public history, and local news decline.
