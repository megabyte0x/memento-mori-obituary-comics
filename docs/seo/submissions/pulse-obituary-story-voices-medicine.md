# Pulse Submission Package

Status: prepared, not sent.

Use only with a fully human first-person story, reflection, or visual that arises from healthcare, caregiving, illness, or a loved one's care experience.

## Source-Checked Route

- Submission page: `https://pulsevoices.org/submit/`
- Best FinalNotes target: `https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-stories/`

## Core Ask

Submit a human story or visual about choosing one concrete detail after clinical facts and public obituary facts no longer feel sufficient. The link is optional context only; do not make the submission depend on a backlink.

## Candidate Category

Choose after a current category check:

- Stories: longer first-person story.
- More Voices: short first-person nonfiction on a current theme.
- New Voices: feature-length first-person story for underrepresented perspectives, if applicable.
- Visuals: rights-cleared image or artwork arising from healthcare experience.

## Candidate Title

`The Detail We Chose After the Chart Went Quiet`

## Story Brief

The piece should follow a real moment: a family member, caregiver, patient, clinician, or loved one has the official medical or death facts but still needs a small detail that restores personhood. That detail becomes the hinge between the record and the obituary story.

## Do Not Send Until

- The story is original, unpublished, and fully human-written.
- The contributor has approved submission.
- Patient and family privacy are handled according to Pulse's current guidance.
- The submission fits the live category, word count, and form requirements.
- The FinalNotes link is used only if contributor-link rules allow it.
