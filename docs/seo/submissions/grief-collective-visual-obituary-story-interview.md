## Why This Qualifies

Grief Collective's current contact page invites blog topic requests and describes two relevant partnership paths: guest blog posts and interview posts. Guest posts are approved by Grief Collective, published with a "Guest post" label, credited to the author, and may include a short blurb linking to the author's site or contact information. Interview posts are for people or causes with a mission related to grief, loss, death, healing, or recovery, and may also include a short blurb link.

The best ask is a short, practical grief-writing post or interview about how families choose one specific moment, scene, object, or gesture when an obituary needs to become a story. The outreach should not ask for SEO value, anchor text, or a promotional feature.
