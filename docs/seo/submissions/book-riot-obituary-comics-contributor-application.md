## Core Ask

Apply to become a Book Riot contributor with a comics/nonfiction-life-writing angle. If accepted, use the monthly pitch process for a natural obituary-comics article.

## Best Angle

Book Riot is a bookish and comics-aware outlet. The strongest path is not "please cover us." It is a contributor application from someone who can write smart short essays about nonfiction comics, visual biography, reading culture, and memory.

## Materials To Prepare

- Resume/CV link.
- Two original Google Docs or Dropbox writing samples.
- PayPal eligibility confirmation.
- Short response on Book Riot's values.
- If accepted later, a monthly pitch around obituary comics as visual biography.

## Do Not Submit Until

- The writing samples are human-written, unpublished, and bookish.
- The submitter is willing to participate as a recurring contributor.
- Any later FinalNotes link is editorial context, not the purpose of the application.
