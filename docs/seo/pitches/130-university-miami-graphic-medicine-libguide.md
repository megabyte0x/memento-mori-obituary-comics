Memento Mori Obituary Comics is a source-backed archive of short visual biographies about people whose lives intersected with death, illness, grief, violence, exile, survival, art, science, or public memory. Each reader page includes comic pages, crawlable summaries, source notes, image captions, and stable URLs. The educator/library page includes discussion prompts around visual biography, grief comics, nonfiction comics, citation, and the ethics of adapting documented lives into sequential art.
