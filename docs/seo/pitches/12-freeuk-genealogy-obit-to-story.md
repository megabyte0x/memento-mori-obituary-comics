## Proposed Article

Title: From Obituary Record to Family History Story

Angle:
Free UK Genealogy's guidance asks for a strong family-history element and practical value. This article should show how to turn an obituary or death record into a richer family-history story without inventing details.

Core sections:

1. Transcribe the obituary or death notice first.
2. Separate facts from interpretation.
3. Identify the missing context: place, occupation, migration, family structure, and cause-of-death language.
4. Use public records to test the story.
5. Decide what can be visualized or described without speculation.
6. Preserve citations so future family historians can verify the work.
