## Why This Fits

Health Story Collaborative invites people to share stories about navigating illness or facing a health challenge, including written narratives, poems, visual art, audio, and other creative media. A visual obituary story can fit only when it is grounded in a real health, caregiving, grief, or end-of-life narrative and handled with consent.
