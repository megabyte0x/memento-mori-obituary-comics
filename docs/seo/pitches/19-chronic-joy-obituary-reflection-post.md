## Compliance Note

Chronic Joy says submissions are by invitation and do not accept AI-generated content. Do not submit generated copy. Use this only as a planning brief for a human-written, personal, faith-compatible post if the publication invites a submission.

## Fit

Chronic Joy asks for story-driven, practical posts with actionable next steps and reflection questions around chronic illness, grief/lament, caregiving, mental illness, chronic pain, and disability. The possible fit is a reflective grief-writing piece, not an SEO pitch.

## Possible Human-Written Angle

Title: Remembering a Life Without Flattening the Pain

Core idea:
A personal essay about using careful prompts to remember a loved one whose life was shaped by illness, disability, caregiving, or chronic pain without reducing them to a diagnosis. The piece should include reflection questions and a short prayer if written for Chronic Joy's format.

## Human Draft Requirements

- Approximately 750 words.
- Personal experience, not generic advice.
- Three reflection questions.
- A short prayer.
- No medical treatment, provider, clinic, product, app, or legal advice.
- No AI-generated submission.
