Working title:
"The Picture We Chose After the Obituary Was Done"

## Pitch / Draft Brief

Narratively is currently looking for sharply focused first-person stories built around a single surprising, unforgettable moment. This angle should be submitted only as a full human-written narrative, not as a backlink request.

The piece would focus on one concrete moment: after the facts of a loved one's obituary were already written, the family had to choose the one image, object, or remembered scene that made the person feel alive again. The story would not explain the whole grief journey. It would stay inside that one decision and what it revealed.
