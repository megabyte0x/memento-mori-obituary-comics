Memento Mori Obituary Comics is a source-backed archive of short visual biographies about people whose lives intersected with death, illness, grief, violence, exile, survival, art, science, or public memory. Each reader page includes comic pages, crawlable summaries, source notes, image captions, and stable URLs. The educator/library page includes discussion prompts for classes, reading groups, and library programming around grief comics, visual biography, nonfiction comics, and the ethics of adapting documented lives into sequential art.
