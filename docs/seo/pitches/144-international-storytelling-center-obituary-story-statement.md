## Statement Prompt

Why storytelling matters: preserving one life after the obituary

## Human Statement Draft Direction

Write a real 1-2 sentence statement from a human submitter. Keep it personal and specific.

Example shape:

Storytelling matters to me because an obituary can say what happened, but a story can preserve the exact gesture, photograph, or sentence that made someone recognizable to the people who loved them.

If the form or profile context allows a supporting URL, use:
https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-stories/

## Submission Notes

- The current form asks for name, email, a short "Why Storytelling Matters to You" statement, a photo upload, and an audio file.
- Decide whether there is a real portrait, family image, or project image that should accompany the statement.
- Record the statement as human audio if submitting.
- Do not force a FinalNotes link into the statement if it would make the submission less natural.
