## Message

Hi History & Genealogy team,

I found the LAPL genealogy guide and its note directing patrons to obituary research resources. One related resource that may be useful after patrons locate an obituary or death notice is a printable obituary story worksheet:

https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-story-worksheet/

The worksheet helps people move from a located record into a careful family-history narrative. It separates verified facts from remembered details, asks for source notes, and includes prompts for choosing one scene or object that can carry the story without adding unsupported claims.

The companion guide is here:

https://www.finalnotes.page/how-to-write-an-obituary-story/

It may fit as an optional writing or family-history prompt for patrons who have already found obituary records and want to preserve the story responsibly.
