I would like to suggest Memento Mori Obituary Comics as a possible resource or review subject for Graphic Medicine.

The project is a source-backed archive of short visual biographies about people whose lives intersected with death, illness, exile, violence, grief, or survival. Each reader page includes comic pages, crawlable summaries, source notes, citations, image captions, and a stable permalink. The educator/library page is written for classroom, reading-group, and library use, with discussion prompts around grief, visual biography, and the ethics of turning documented lives into comics.
