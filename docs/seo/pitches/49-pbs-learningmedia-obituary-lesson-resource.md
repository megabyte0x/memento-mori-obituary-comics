## Message

Hi PBS LearningMedia team,

I found the PBS History Detectives "Written in Stone" classroom resource while researching lesson plans that use historical evidence, cemetery records, and obituary writing.

One supplemental resource that may fit evidence-based writing or media-literacy collections is this obituary lesson plan:

https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-lesson-plan/

It asks students to compare death notices, eulogies, reported obituary articles, and visual obituaries, then draft a source-backed life story using verified facts and one concrete detail. It includes classroom steps, an assessment checklist, and visual obituary examples with source trails.

If useful, it could fit as an outside resource for history, ELA, journalism, or media literacy teachers.
