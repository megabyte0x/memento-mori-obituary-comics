## About Page

Title:

```text
About Borrowed Time Dispatch
```

Body:

```text
Borrowed Time Dispatch is the email edition of Memento Mori Obituary Comics: short illustrated obituaries about people who faced death, interruption, illness, captivity, or ruin, then made work that outlasted the moment.

Each dispatch points back to a permanent comic page with image pages, a PDF, story notes, and sources. The tone is plain and unsentimental: no productivity theater, no motivational gloss, just the moment death entered the room and what the person made afterward.

Start with the public archive:

https://www.finalnotes.page/

Read the editorial method:

https://www.finalnotes.page/about/
```

## Sponsorship Positioning

Use sponsorships only when they fit the tone of the archive.

Good fits:

- Books and independent presses.
- Research, reading, and note-taking tools.
- History, biography, philosophy, and art projects.
- Estate-planning education.
- Memorial and remembrance products with restrained copy.

Avoid:

- Generic growth tools.
- Loud crypto promotion.
- Wellness products that flatten death into a lifestyle brand.
- Anything that would make the obituary subject feel like ad inventory.

## Crypto Support Positioning

Keep the first Substack experience reader-first. The public site can carry crypto support without making the email feel like a wallet prompt.

Use this sentence when needed:

```text
Readers who prefer crypto can support the public archive through the site; agents can also request the latest PDF through the x402 endpoint.
```

Do not paste private keys, Vercel tokens, wallet seed phrases, or payment API secrets into Substack.
