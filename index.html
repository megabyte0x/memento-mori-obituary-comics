⏳Diagnosed with motor neurone disease around his 21st birthday and given roughly two years to live before decades of later work on singularities, black holes, and public science.

Louis Zamperini

Olympic runner, World War II airman, survivor, and public witness to endurance after the Pacific nearly erased him.

⏳Survived a 1943 Pacific crash, 47 days adrift, and nearly two years as a Japanese prisoner of war before his later public testimony and forgiveness work.

Mexican painter whose self-portraits made pain, identity, the body, and death modern art's evidence.
